Macbeth's Fate

Help

Instructions

Determine Macbeth's fate by making decisions for him. Type a command and hit enter.

Commands

There are two commands: kill and no-kill.

kill

The "kill" command will be followed by a character's name that Macbeth is approaching or is faced with making a decision about, and Macbeth will attempt to kill the character. For example one might type "kill Banquo".

no-kill

The "no-kill" command is the command used when Macbeth chooses not to kill anyone. It also can be thought of as the listen-to command in some scenarios.

Description:
Macbeth is walking home from a victory with Banquo when they approach three witches -- they are creepy. What does Macbeth do?
